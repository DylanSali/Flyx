BNE immigration — ~12 min departures · ~25 min arrivals
Step by step.
From check-in to arrival — tap each stage
1
Check-in
2–3 hrs before departure
›
Online
Opens 24–48h before — do it from your phone to pick your seat early and avoid queues.
Bag drop
Separate from check-in — follow airport signs. Have your booking reference ready.
Weight
Checked at drop-off. Overweight fees: A$20–50 per kg. Weigh at home first.
2
Baggage drop
After check-in
›
Tag
Printed at check-in — attach it to your bag before joining the drop queue.
Fragile
Declare at the counter so items are labelled for careful handling.
Lock
Use a TSA-approved lock — security can open it without breaking it.
3
Security screening
Allow 20–30 min
›
Electronics
Laptop and large devices go in a separate tray — remove before the belt.
Liquids
Max 100ml per container in one clear zip-lock bag. Buy water after security.
Wear
Some airports ask for shoes and belt off. Watch what people ahead of you do.
4
Departure immigration
BNE — SmartGate available
›
SmartGate
Use the automated lane if you have an e-passport. Much faster than manual.
Have ready
Passport, boarding pass, and student visa grant letter on your phone.
Questions
Routine — purpose of travel, how long, return date. See Checklist tab for full list.
5
Transit / layover
At your connecting airport
›
Timing
Under 90 min is tight for international connections. Ask staff if unsure.
Changi
4h+ at SIN? Butterfly garden, Jewel waterfall, free shower at transit hotel — all airside.
Food
Transit airports often have better and cheaper food than the plane.
6
Boarding
Gate closes 20–30 min early
›
Gate
Be at the gate 45 min before departure — not at departure time.
Overhead
Overhead space fills fast — board early if you have a large carry-on.
Phone
Flight mode once doors close. Download movies and offline maps before boarding.
7
Arrival & customs
At your home country
›
Order
Immigration first — then baggage claim — then customs declaration.
Bags
Check screens for your flight number and belt. Allow 20–40 min for bags to appear.
Customs
Declare electronics, cash over limit, and any food or plant items. When in doubt, declare.
Onward
Pre-book your ride or arrange pickup before you land — airport taxi queues can be long.
